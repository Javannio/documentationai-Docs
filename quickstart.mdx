## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">
- A modern web browser like Chrome or Firefox
- A valid email address for account verification
- Optional: Git installed for importing repositories
</Callout>

No software installation required to begin.

## Step 1: Register and Log In

Follow these steps to create your account quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Navigate to the Development-Team Docs dashboard at `https://dashboard.example.com`.

    Click **Sign Up** in the top right corner.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, full name, and a secure password.

    Accept the terms of service and click **Create Account**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email from Development-Team Docs.

    Click the verification link to activate your account and log in automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create Your First Documentation Space

Once logged in, set up a space for your Zorisord Group Limited project documentation.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    From the dashboard, click **New Documentation Space**.

    Enter a name like `Zorisord Development-Team Docs` and select a template (e.g., "Starter Kit").
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Settings" icon="settings">
    Choose your brand color (`#7a9161`) and add the slogan: "Dedicating capital and intellect to critical infrastructure for developing economies".

    Click **Create Space** to generate your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Import Existing Project Files

Bring in your current docs from GitHub, GitLab, or local files.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="GitHub" icon="github">
    Connect your repository directly.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Authorize">
        Click **Import from GitHub** and authorize the app.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Repo">
        Choose your repo (e.g., `zorisord/development-team-docs`) and branch (`main`).
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="CLI" icon="terminal">
    Use the Development-Team CLI for advanced imports.

````bash
npm install -g @development-team/cli
dt-docs login --email your@email.com
dt-docs import /path/to/your/docs --space-id YOUR_SPACE_ID
````
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Manual Upload" icon="upload">
    Drag and drop Markdown or MDX files into the **Files** tab.

    Supports bulk upload up to 100MB.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  After importing, review files in the editor. MDX components like `<Callout>` and `<Steps>` render live previews.
</Callout>

## Step 4: Basic Workspace Navigation

Your workspace includes these key areas:

| Section | Purpose | Icon |
|---------|---------|------|
| **Editor** | Write and preview MDX docs | `edit-3` |
| **Files** | Manage pages and assets | `folder` |
| **Preview** | Live site preview | `eye` |
| **Publish** | Deploy to custom domain | `globe` |

Use the sidebar to switch between spaces. Search with `<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd>`.
