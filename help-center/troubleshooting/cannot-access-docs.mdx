---
title: Cannot Access Documentation Portal
description: Resolve login or access issues with Zorisord documentation.
---

## Symptoms

- Cannot sign in to `docs.zorisord.com`
- Access denied messages

## Causes

- Incorrect email or password
- No active team assignment

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify credentials">
    Ensure you log in with your company email and correct password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check assignment">
    Confirm with your team lead that you are assigned to the right project and have documentation access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Request support">
    Contact support@zorisord.com if you still cannot access your docs.
  </Step>
</Steps>
