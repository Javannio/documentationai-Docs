## Overview

To start using Zorisord Group Limited | Development-Team's documentation portal:

1. Sign in at `https://docs.zorisord.com` with your provided company email.
2. Once logged in, access your team's assigned documentation spaces.
3. Use the sidebar to search or browse existing project docs.
4. Click your project to review guidelines and resources.
5. Need a new space? Ask your project lead to create or assign you to one.
6. Contact support if you have any access issues.
